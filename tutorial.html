First Kickdrum

Locate the first kickdrum using the turntable. Now hold PLAY and press CUE button and release. This saves that starting point to your PLAY and CUE button. When you press play it will start from that first kickdrum.

Home

CUE button

The CUE button only plays music if you push it or pressed it down. A good technique I use is tapping the CUE button in rhythm with the song playing. then I switch tapping the cue button to push the play button at the first kickdrum of the outro. This gets you remarkably close to a perfect beat match. Use the wheel on the turntables to adjust the last percent to beatmatch the two songs together to make them sound clean and avoid noise cancelation.

preparing for the mix

64 bars into outro there is a perfect window for us to start playing our right track. find the rythmn by tapping QUE and at 64 bars switch and press PLAY.

Now the tracks are playing side by side. Now its time to mix them together so the crowd can hear too! Start with Turning down the right bass knob to 0%
